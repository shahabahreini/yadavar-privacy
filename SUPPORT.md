## Common Questions

### Where is my data stored?

Your events and app settings are stored locally on your device. Optional backups are created only when you choose to create them.

### Can I use Yadavar without Google Drive?

Yes. Google Drive backup is optional. The core calendar and reminder features work without connecting a Google account.

### What Google permission does Yadavar request?

Yadavar uses the restricted Google Drive `drive.appdata` scope for optional Drive backup. This scope allows the app to access its own hidden app data folder, not your regular Drive files.

### What happens if I forget an encrypted backup passphrase?

Encrypted backups cannot be restored without the passphrase. The passphrase is not sent to the developer.

### How do I delete my data?

Delete events in the app, delete backup files you created, clear app data through your device settings, or uninstall the app. You can also revoke Google access from your Google Account permissions page.
