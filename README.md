## 🌟 About Yadavar

**Yadavar (یادآور)** is a feature-rich **Persian (Jalali/Shamsi) calendar and reminder** application available on iOS, Android, and macOS. It bridges the gap between the Persian calendar and modern digital life — helping Persian-speaking users manage their schedules, track Persian holidays, and set reminders, all within a beautiful and intuitive interface.

> **یادآور** — Persian for *"reminder"* or *"one who reminds"* — is built with your privacy and productivity in mind.

## ✨ Key Features

- 📅 **Full Persian (Jalali/Shamsi) Calendar** — accurate date conversion and display
- 🎉 **Persian Holidays** — automatic import of Iranian national and religious holidays
- 🔔 **Local Reminders** — set notifications for any event, entirely on-device
- ☁️ **Google Drive Backup** — optional, user-initiated backup to your private Drive app folder
- 🔐 **Encrypted Backups** — optional AES-256-GCM encryption for your backup files
- 📴 **Fully Offline** — works without internet (holiday sync requires connectivity)
- 🌙 **Dark Mode** — beautiful light and dark themes
- 🖥️ **Multi-Platform** — iOS, Android, and macOS support

## 🔒 Privacy at a Glance

Yadavar is built with a **privacy-first philosophy**:

- ✅ All your calendar data stays **on your device**
- ✅ No analytics, no ads, no third-party tracking SDKs
- ✅ Google Drive backups go to a **hidden app folder only you can access**
- ✅ Holiday data is fetched via **anonymous API requests** — no personal data sent
- ✅ Encryption is **optional and user-controlled**

See the full [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.
