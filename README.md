# Privacy Policy for Yadavar (یادآور)

**Last Updated: February 10, 2026**

## Introduction

Yadavar ("یادآور") is a Persian event reminder application with Shamsi calendar support. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our app works.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Collect

- **Events**: Birthday, ceremony, memorial, national, religious, anniversary, and custom events you create
- **Event Details**: Dates, categories, notes, and reminder preferences you set
- **App Settings**: Your preferences for notifications, Georgian date display, and category customization

### How Data is Stored

- **100% Local Storage**: All your data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage
- **No Cloud Servers**: We do not operate any servers or cloud storage
- **No Accounts**: No user accounts, usernames, passwords, or login systems
- **Complete Privacy**: Your events and personal information never leave your device

## Permissions Used

### Local Notifications

- **Purpose**: To remind you about upcoming events
- **Scope**: Notifications are generated locally on your device
- **Control**: You can enable/disable notifications in app settings

### Storage Access

- **Purpose**: To save your events, settings, and create local backup files
- **Scope**: Only accesses app-specific storage on your device
- **Control**: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

### Network Access (Optional)

- **Purpose**: Optional feature to fetch Persian national and religious holidays
- **What We Send**: No personal data is sent. Only anonymous API requests to public Persian calendar services
- **Sources**: Persian Holidays API (basemax.github.io) and Persian Calendar API
- **Control**: This feature is optional and can be disabled
- **Your Data**: Your personal events are never transmitted over the network

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:

- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data to anyone
- Send your data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking
- Display advertisements
- Collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone, etc.)

## Backup and Export

- **Local Backups**: You can create encrypted backup files stored on your device
- **File Sharing**: When you use the backup feature, files are shared through your device's native sharing mechanism
- **Encryption**: Backups can be password-protected with AES-256-GCM encryption
- **Your Control**: You decide where to save or share backup files

## Children's Privacy

Since our app:

- Does not collect personal information
- Stores all data locally
- Does not communicate with external servers (except optional public holiday API)
- Does not require registration

It is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Parents can review the app's functionality and permissions before allowing their children to use it.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention**: Data is stored on your device until you delete it
- **Manual Deletion**: You can delete individual events or all data within the app
- **Complete Removal**: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the app and on our distribution page with a new "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services

### Persian Calendar APIs (Optional)

When you choose to fetch automatic events, the app makes requests to:

- Persian Holidays API (GitHub Pages - basemax.github.io)
- Persian Calendar API

These requests:

- Are anonymous and do not include personal data
- Only retrieve public Persian calendar dates and holidays
- Are made directly from your device
- Can be disabled at any time

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all your events and settings within the app
- **Modify**: Edit or delete any event at any time
- **Export**: Create backup files of your data
- **Delete**: Remove all data by clearing the app or uninstalling

## Security

- **Local Encryption**: Backup files can be encrypted with your chosen password
- **No Transmission**: Since data stays on device, there's no transmission security risk
- **Device Security**: Your data security depends on your device's security measures
