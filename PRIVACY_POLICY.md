# Privacy Policy for Yadavar

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026  
**Public page:** https://shahabahreini.github.io/yadavar-privacy/privacy.html

Yadavar (یادآور) is a Persian calendar and reminder app developed by Shahab Bahreini Jangjoo. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Yadavar stores your calendar events, event notes, categories, reminder settings, and app preferences locally on your device. This information is not sent to a Yadavar server.

## Backups and Export

You may choose to create local backup/export files. You control where those files are saved or shared through your device or operating system.

Yadavar also supports optional Google Drive backup. When you connect Google Drive, the app uses Google OAuth and the restricted `drive.appdata` scope to store backup files in the hidden app-specific folder for Yadavar. The app also requests Google account email information so it can show which account is connected.

Backup encryption is optional. If enabled, backup files are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before they are saved or uploaded. Your backup passphrase is not sent to the developer.

## Notifications

Reminder notifications are local notifications scheduled by your device. Yadavar does not use remote push notifications to deliver reminder content.

## Holiday Data

If you choose to fetch public Persian calendar events or holidays, the app sends network requests to public holiday data sources such as `basemax.github.io` and `api.persian-calendar.ir`. These requests are used to download public calendar data and do not include your personal events.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not sell your personal information.
- We do not use advertising SDKs.
- We do not use third-party tracking SDKs.
- We do not operate a Yadavar server that stores your calendar events.

## Data Deletion

You can delete events inside the app. You can remove local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data through your device settings. Google Drive backup files can be deleted from within the app where supported, and Google access can be revoked from your Google Account permissions page.

## Children's Privacy

Yadavar is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as Yadavar changes. The latest version will be posted with an updated date.
